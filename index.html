The Minimalissimo newsletter is sponsored by aprile, the hanging chair.

Minimalissimo is an independent print and digital magazine founded in 2009 and dedicated to minimalism. For more than a decade it has showcased a curated selection of art, architecture, interiors, furniture, lighting and product design.



MANIFESTO


We curate to reflect who we are and who we aspire to be. Guided by minimalism and simplicity, we appreciate brutalism, geometry and honest design. Our vision is future-focused while respecting the past.


PRINCIPLES


  1. aesthetic: design for love, not only understanding
  2. collaborative: create beyond your own ability
  3. experimental: think three-dimensionally
  4. geometric: use pure forms to emphasise precision
  5. honest: build with integrity
  6. modular: reuse building blocks for flexibility
  7. sustainable: design for people and the planet (and write clean code)
  8. translucent: build in public, by appointment only
  9. understated: be practical rather than pretty
  10. unified: design for continual and consistent expression
